HAPPY NEW YEAR HOLIDAY 2026

On 1st January 2026, during the New Year holiday, we celebrated the day with over 120 unhoused children at Uhuru Gardens, Kericho. The occasion was marked by sharing joy, encouragement, and fellowship. The children were provided with refreshments and bars of soap. After the activity, the remaining bars of soap were donated to Kericho Maximum Prison as part of our continued community support efforts. The activity reflected our commitment to compassion, dignity, and service to vulnerable members of the community.

KAPKWEN KITOBEN CHILDREN'S HOME

On 31st December 2025, during the end-of-year period, our organization was represented by the Secretary in Bomet County at Kapkwen Kitoben Children’s Home. The event was hosted by our partners, Beacon of Hearts CBO, and aimed at supporting and sharing with children at the home. Our participation strengthened collaboration and partnership in community service and child welfare initiatives.

CHRISTMAS CELEBRATIONS WITH UNHOUSED CHILDREN @UHURU GARDENS KERICHO

On 25th December 2025, we celebrated Christmas with unhoused children at Uhuru Gardens, Kericho. We shared refreshments together and carried out deworming for all the children, totaling 86 beneficiaries. In addition, we were able to purchase sandals for one child. Although we wished to provide sandals to more children, this was not possible due to financial limitations. The activity brought joy to the children and reinforced our commitment to supporting vulnerable members of the community.

FREE MEDICAL CAMP @KEONGO PRI SCHOOL

We hosted a free medical camp at Keongo Primary School on 17th December 2025. The event recorded over 400 registered attendees from the community. Due to a shortage of medical personnel, only 80 patients were attended to. Medical services were provided by doctors and health practitioners from Equity Afya Medical Center and the University of Kabianga Nursing Department. Despite the challenges, the activity was successful and demonstrated strong community interest in accessible healthcare services.
