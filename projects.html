CHEPKOWA TEA BUYING CENTRE STALLED AGAIN

In 2017, a major environmental awareness campaign was launched in Mutare District, Kenya, to raise awareness of the need for responsible and sustainable mining operations. The campaign was aimed at educating local communities about the environmental risks posed by mining operations and the importance of sustainable management of natural resources. The campaign included a series of public meetings, posters, and television and radio advertisements.

STOP SEXUAL OFFENCES KNOW THE LAW

Voices for Justice CBO transforms communities through education, advocacy, and action and today, we are educating you to understand that rape and defilement are serious criminal offences in Kenya, carrying very heavy penalties under the Sexual Offences Act, 2006 and the Penal Code of Kenya.
