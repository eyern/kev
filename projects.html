Two Major Justice Wins for Our Organisation!

Our Organisation is celebrating two life imprisonment convictions victories made possible through committed follow-up and strong community support. In Bureti Constituency, Chemosot Ward, Justice Kipsigie defiled a one-year-old child in April. Our Anti-GBV members led by Margaret Ruto visited the child in hospital and followed up the case. In September, Kericho Law Courts sentenced him to life imprisonment. A big win. In a similar case followed up by our Organisation in Kipkelion West, Lisirwa, David Kemei assaulted his wife twice. Our members rushed to Siloam Hospital and offered support. On Thursday 20/11/2025, Kericho Law Courts sentenced him to life imprisonment. Another big win. Together we can end GBV.
